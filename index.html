If you're looking for some simple blocky fun but with a fresh coat of paint. This is the place for you!

💌 Why This Server Exists

CinnamonCream started as a cozy passion project—a warm, welcoming digital space where friends could gather, build, and survive together. It's maintained with love, caffeine, and the occasional debugging session at 3 AM. The goal is simple: create a fun, engaging survival experience that stays true to Minecraft's core while adding just enough spice to keep things interesting. No pay-to-win, no drama, just good vibes and blocky adventures. Welcome to our sweet little corner of the Minecraft universe!

🛠️ Features & Mayhem

This isn't your average vanilla Minecraft server. We've carefully curated a collection of features that enhance the survival experience without straying too far from the core gameplay. Prepare for surprises, challenges, and plenty of laughs.

🍀
Lucky Blocks

Randomized drops that bring chaos and fun. Will you get diamonds or disaster? Only one way to find out!

🥘
New Food Recipes

Tasty Supplies datapack adds delicious vanilla-friendly foods. Expand your culinary horizons!

✨
New Enchantments

Enchant Encore gives you new ways to empower your gear. Discover powerful combinations!

🌍
Fully Explorable Worlds

Overworld, Nether, and End are all open for exploration. Venture far and build your empire!

🛠️
Veinminer

Mine smarter, not harder. Break entire ore veins with one swing. Your back will thank you!

🪓
Timber

Chop an entire tree with your axe in one go. Less time clicking logs, more time building.

🐍
Ground Mob Spawning

Mobs can emerge from the ground beneath your feet. Stay alert and watch your step!

🛡️
Land Claiming

Protect your builds with Grief Prevention and keep unwanted hands off your stuff. Great for long-term bases and shared projects.

🔐
Account Login System

Offline-mode players can register and log in to secure their progress, while premium players can enable automatic login.

🌲
Random Teleport

Use /rtp to get dropped somewhere fresh and start your next survival journey away from the main crowd.

🪣
Villager In A Bucket

Pick up villagers with a bucket and move them where you need them. Trading hall logistics are much less painful now.

🏠
Useful Player Commands

Daily survival essentials like /sethome, /home, /tpa, /tpahere, and /ndailyrewards are ready to use.

🎮
Java & Bedrock Compatible

Play from any device! Both Java and Bedrock editions are supported, so everyone can join the fun.
